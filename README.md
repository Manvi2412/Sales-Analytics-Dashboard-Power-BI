# Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI


## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze and visualize sales performance across products, regions, and sales channels. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, identify high-performing segments, and uncover actionable insights for data-driven decision-making.

The dataset includes transactional sales data covering multiple product categories, cities, states, and sales channels.

---

## Key Metrics

* Total Revenue: ₹248.86M
* Average Order Value: ₹497.73K
* Total Quantity Sold: 16K+

---

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Sales Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance:

* Revenue distribution across regions
* Top-performing products by revenue
* Sales contribution by channel (Online, Retail, Distributor)
* Monthly sales trend analysis
* City-wise revenue distribution

### 2. Product Insights

Focuses on product-level performance:

* Revenue contribution by product category
* Product-wise revenue comparison using treemap
* Relationship between quantity sold and revenue
* Monthly revenue trends
* Target vs actual revenue comparison

### 3. Region & Channel Insights

Analyzes geographic and channel-based performance:

* State-wise and region-wise revenue distribution
* Sales channel performance across regions
* Identification of top-performing regions
* City-level revenue contribution

---

## Key Business Insights

* The North region generates the highest revenue, indicating strong market penetration and demand concentration in this region.
* Electronics category contributes the majority share of total revenue, making it the primary revenue driver for the business.
* Online sales channel outperforms Retail and Distributor channels, highlighting the growing importance of digital sales platforms.
* Revenue is concentrated in a few key cities such as Delhi and Mumbai, suggesting opportunities for expansion in underperforming regions.
* Higher sales quantity does not always correspond to higher revenue, indicating variability in product pricing and margins.
* Monthly sales show fluctuations, with certain months significantly outperforming others, suggesting seasonal demand patterns.
